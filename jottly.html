The Making of Jott.ly

Problem

Create a logo and a landing page for the newly formed startup caleld Jott.ly. It is a simple SaaS (Software as a Service) web application focused on allowing users to sign up and create an account, make simple content pages, and provide a way to collaborate with others within their organization.

Method in the Madness

Since Jott.ly is a lightweight web application, I wanted to create a design that mirrored the nature of the application. When designing I tried to keep all design aspects simple and clean. I kept the copy short and straight to the point. I used graphics sparingly. The message I wanted to get across was that the application uses modern solutions to solve a simple problem. The color palette of the sea green and orange was chosen to give the product a more modern look and feel.
